So, why do we do this?

Our Organization was established in 2023 for children who left without
parental care for various reasons. In that time, we have helped over 5,000 children.
This is 5,000 children who have been given a new life.
This is 5,000 children who know the Price of Life.

​ We can’t help everyone,
but everyone can help someone

Can life be changed?

I know people who organize meetings with their friends and
go to clean the garbage from beaches and forests. I know those who
save hundreds of homeless animals every day. There are also those who care about lonely old people.
These are all very good people. We do not notice when they do this, but
we know that without them it would be much worse.

As well as we are changing life on our Earth for the better. Children that no one needed became part
of our family. We teach them to love animals, not to litter around themselves, as well as our children
take care of the elderly in the area where they live.
